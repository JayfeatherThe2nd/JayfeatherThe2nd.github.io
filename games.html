Cattails


Cattails is a game where you play as a cat. There are three main wild cat groups you can join. Hunt and fight and make sure you don't die! Increase your relationship by giving gifts and chatting with other eligible cats to confess to them and have kittens! (The cats eligible as mates have a heart next to their dialogue.)

Customize your character and jump into a relaxing island life simulator! Decorate your house, chat with villagers,(animal friends)and grow crops! There are lots of secrets to discover and plenty of fun to be had in this entertaining game available on the Nintendo Switch!
